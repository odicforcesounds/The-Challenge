Just Another Open Door

I am the Gate Keeper, the Portal and the Path

Pay attention, You may find new doors open...

Do You wish to come with me?

Stay where You are and read, and feel so You learn!

Doors can open or close.

Do You know what doors give You?

Experience, while You gave Your Self to it.

I am a door, while You are a door as well.

I talk to my self, while you read, I talk to You.

Decisions are not always the best, while is only me or You.

Making wrong decisions can lead us to learn, but is not logic that it will.

Making good decisions can lead us to be secure, but is not logic to be secure.

Security is needed, as information to care decisions.

Limits are needed, as decisions which care information.

Pay attention to your decisions, they are not part of your expressions.
