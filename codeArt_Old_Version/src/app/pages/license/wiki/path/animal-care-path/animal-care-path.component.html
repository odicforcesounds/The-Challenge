The process of finding a way to give a solution is not always faster and not always possible as it cost money to use health care tools and time. This process cost a lot of money to this persons, but they would try to do something without money, as more they could. So, since there is so much in cost, poeple who pay attention in this topics shall be rewarded. People can make a big report of the situation and how they solved. Is not by the will to find mistakes in each one try to solve a problem, but to find that people really tried, then they have the merit of earing the retribution. Social media network are very important on this subjects.
